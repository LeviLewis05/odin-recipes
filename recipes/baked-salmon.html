Baked Salmon

Baked Salmon with herbs and lemons

Baked Salmon is a great healthy option for dinner. You will feel fancy after making it wthout having too get a culinary deegre. This is because of the simplicity of the recipe. I guarntee if you are a fan of salmon you will love this recipe and it will be one of your go too dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salmon
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 2 lemons
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • fresh parsley

Baking Directions

  1. Preheat oven too 375 deegres
  2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil
  3. Pour olive oil on the aluminum foil
  4. Place lemon slices and parsley on the baking sheet
  5. Place salmon on top of the baking sheet
  6. Put the remainng lemon slices and parsley ontop of salmon
  7. Juice the second lemon ontop till satisfyed
  8. Season with salt and pepper
  9. Fold the aluminum foil over the salmon
  10. Bake for 30 minutes
