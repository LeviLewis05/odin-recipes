Pork Egg Rolls

Pork Egg Rolls with cabbage and carrots

These egg rolls are great for many occasions such as for a snack and for a good side dish maybe even with the other dishes on this site. These egg rolls come loaded with carrots, cabbage and pork with a crisppy shell outside.

Ingredients

  • Ground Pork
  • Egg Roll Wrappers
  • Ground Ginger
  • Garlic Powder
  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Flour
  • Peanut oil

Cooking Directions

  1. Season the pork with ginger and garlic powder
  2. Cook pork in skillet for about 10 minutes
  3. Mix pork, carrots and cabbage together
  4. Mix flour and water into a paste
  5. Start heating oil in a skillet
  6. While heating roll up the egg roll filling with the wrappers
  7. Seal the rolls with the flour paste
  8. Fry egg rolls for about 10 minutes
